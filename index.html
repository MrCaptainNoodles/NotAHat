How to Play

  • Goal: Finish the game with the lowest score (fewest penalty cards).
  • Memorize: At the start, memorize everyone's face-up cards.
  • Draw & Pass: On your turn, draw a card. Pass your old card face-down in the direction shown on the back of the new card.
  • Declare: Tell the receiver what item you are passing them. You can tell the truth or bluff!
  • Respond: The receiver can Accept it (they now hold it) or Challenge you.
  • Challenge: If they challenge, the card is revealed. If you lied, you get a penalty. If you told the truth, they get a penalty!
  • Round End: Once someone gets 3 penalties, the round ends and those cards are added to everyone's total score.
